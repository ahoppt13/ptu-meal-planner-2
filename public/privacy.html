Privacy Policy — PT:U Meal Planner

Last updated: 7 July 2026

The PT:U Meal Planner ("the app") is operated by PT:U Personal Training, Wokingham, United Kingdom ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We keep it in plain English on purpose.

What we collect

Details you give us. Your name, email address and fitness goal when you start a plan; and, if you create an account, your email address and a password (stored securely by our authentication provider — we never see your password).

Health and body information. To calculate your calories and build your meal plan, the app asks for your age, gender, weight, height, activity level, dietary preference, food allergies, and optionally health conditions (such as high blood pressure, high cholesterol or type 2 diabetes). By entering this information you consent to us processing it for the sole purpose of generating your personalised meal plans and saving your preferences to your account. We never use your health information for marketing or share it with advertisers.

App activity. If you have an account, we store your saved preferences, favourite meals and hidden meals so they're there when you come back.

AI Chef inputs. If you use the AI Chef feature, the ingredient text and/or photo you submit is sent securely to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate your recipe, along with your calorie target, diet and allergy settings. We do not store your photos.

How we use your information

We use your information to: generate and personalise your meal plans and shopping lists; operate your account; email you your meal plan when you request it; and contact you about PT:U coaching, services and offers relevant to the goal you told us about. Every marketing email includes a way to opt out, and you can ask us to stop at any time.

Who we share it with

We don't sell your information. We use a small number of trusted services to run the app: Supabase (secure accounts and data storage), Netlify (hosting), Google (secure storage of the contact details you submit), and Anthropic (processing AI Chef requests). Each processes data only as needed to provide their service.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you can ask us at any time to: see the information we hold about you, correct it, delete it, or stop using it for marketing. To do any of these, email us at info@pt-u.co.uk and we'll act on it promptly. You can also delete saved meals and preferences directly in the app.

Data retention & security

Account data is kept while your account is active and deleted on request. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored with providers that maintain industry-standard security.

Age

The app is intended for adults. If you are under 18, please use it only with a parent or guardian's involvement, and note that calorie targets are calculated using adult formulas.

Health disclaimer

The app provides general nutrition information calculated from established formulas and peer-reviewed guidance. It is not medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional before making significant dietary changes, particularly if you have a medical condition.

Changes & contact

If we change this policy we'll update this page and the date above. Questions? Email info@pt-u.co.uk or visit www.pt-u.co.uk.
